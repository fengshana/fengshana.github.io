Learn & Talk I

Chapter 1 Shopping

Lesson 4 Shopping for a TV

at the bottom of the following pages:T1=teaching instructions;T=teacher;S=student

Contents

  • part A. Let’s Learn (12 mins)

  • part B. Let’s Talk (12 mins)

  • part C. Review (1 min)

Learning Objectives

part A. Let’s Learn

Vocabulary (4 mins)

warranty a written promise from a company to repair or replace a product that develops a fault within a particular period of time 保修单，保修卡，保修服务 e.g. The TV has a 3-year warranty.
best-seller a new bool or other product that has sold a great number of copies. 热卖品，畅销品 e.g. As a writer, he wants to write a best-seller.
offer to provide or supply something. 提供 e.g. He offered me a glass of water.
delivery the act of taking goods,letters,parcels,etc. to people's houses or places of work 运送，递送 e.g. They offer free delivery, so it's a good deal. deliver(v.) The letter was delivered yesterday,

Dialog

Role-play the dialog with your teacher twice,and learn the highlighted expressions by heart.和老师一起分角色朗读两遍一下对话。熟记标亮部分的短语和表达

（John and Anne are shopping for a TV.）

Anne: How about this one?
John: I like this brand ,and the price isn’t too high.
（They start to ask the salesperson for information.）
John: How long is the warranty for this TV?
Salesperson: It comes with a 2-year parts(零件)warranty. It’s also the best-seller now.
Anne: Do you offer free delivery?
Anne: Yes, we do.

part B. Let’s Talk

Conversation

Suppose you’re shopping for appliances. Use th vocabulary and expressions you’ve learned in part A to complete the following conversation. 假设你正在选购电器。运用在第一部分学到的词汇和表达将一下情境对话补充完整

Hints
delivery
warranty
How long is the warranty for …?
best-seller
offer

A: Excuse me. How along is warranty for this air conditioner(空调) ?
B: Three years. It’s the best-seller now. We also offer free delivery.
A: But there’s an air conditioner of this brand in the living room of my house,and it doesn’t work very well recently.
B: We’ll offer free repair(维修) if it’s still under warranty.
A: Fine.

Discussion

Talk with your teacher about the following question. Refer to the hints if necessary.和老师一起讨论一下问题。可以参考方框中的提示

  1. How often do you watch TV? When do you usually watch TV?

Hints:

every day / usually / from time to time(偶尔) / never …
in the evening(morning ,afternoon) / at the noon / on weekends …

  1. What kind of TV programs(节目) do you like ? Why?

Hints:

news -> learn what’s happening around the world …
talk shows(访谈节目) -> know more about the life of famous people …
variety shows (综艺节目) -> funny,interesting ,relaxing(令人放松的) …

  1. What will you consider(考虑) when buying an appliance(电器)？

Hints:

brands (what brands?) / prices (how much?) / warranty(how long?) 、 delivery (free or not?) ….

In this lesson,you’ve learned

  1. words about “buying appliances”

warranty
best-seller
offer
delivery

  • commonly used expressions for buying appliances

How long is the warranty for this TV?
Do you offer free delivery?

  • how to talk about TV and other appliances
